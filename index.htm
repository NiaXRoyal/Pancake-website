    For your ingredients you will need

  • 2 Cups of flour
  • 1 Teaspoon of baking powder
  • 2 Table spoons of caster sugar
  • Greek yoghurt
  • Half a cup of milk
  • 1 large egg
  • four ripe bannanas
  • Whipped cream
  • And last but not least 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

How To Make Bananna Pancakes

To make pancakes you first need to get all of your dry ingredients (greek yoghurt,flour,sugar and baking powder).

After that put your wet ingredients together (egg,vanilla extract).

Mix both your wet and dry ingredients together.

Once you've done that add your milk (if neccesary)after you have mixed your wet and dry ingredients together

Once theyre all mixed it should look like cake batter(keep in mind the that it DOES NOTtaste like cake batter (like at all) so dont try to lick the bowl or what not after your done

Let your batter rest for a while before the next step;put a tea towl over it to give the baking powder time to rise

Once thats nicley prepped chop two of your banannas into little squares and put mix into mixture after that proceed to crush the bannanas and mix simultaniously

After that get your next two bannanas and slice them normally and mix into mixture

Now that your batter is ready prep your pan;heat it up and spray with a little oil (so that the pancake dosnt stick)before making your pancake

On each side of the pancake bake for about 3-4mins (but do check from time to time)

AND YOUR DONE YAYYYYY!
